Now who are you again?

I'm just a 14-year-old person doing what I really enjoy.

I learned Luau in 2020, but started learning Javascript in late 2024 as I didn't want to deal with the limitations of the big Luau-powered platforms (just Roblox).

I also learned graphic design around late 2023 after trying out Vectornator, but I use Inkscape or GIMP when I'm not near a Mac. Me and Canva kind of have beef.

I started video editing in early 2024 using CapCut, but I never used any templates, instead making my own when possible. I learned Kdenlive after switching to GNU/Linux, and that's what I do most of my work on nowadays.

You can also visit my personal website at ws4k.net if you wanted to view some of my other projects.
